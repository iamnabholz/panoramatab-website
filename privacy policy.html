We only use coordinates provided by the browser (or yourself) to get information about the weather. This information is used to make a one time call to the weather API service, it's not stored anywhere outside your browser or shared with any third-party services.

The Panorama Tab extension requires a certain amount of permissions so you can enjoy its full potential. For security reasons we use the narrowest permissions available to still provide you the full functionality. These permissions are:

  • topSites: To show your most visited sites.
  • https://: To make network request for the services.
  • Reddit: Will connect to get posts

  • OpenWeatherMap: To get weather informaton
