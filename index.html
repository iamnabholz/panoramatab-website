Panorama Tab

Available On

Panorama Tab replaces your browser new tab with a unique design and set of features that you can customize to fit your style.

Main Features

Current Weather

Get a quick glance at the current temperature in your location

provided by opwenweathermap.org

Top Sites

Easy access to your most visited websites and apps

Background Images

Customize the background with curated images from any category you want

provided by unsplash.com

Reddit Posts

See hot, new or rising posts from your favorite subreddit without an account

Customization

Disable or change the way section are displayed
